Botika · Mobile App
Team Lead Mobile App Healthcare UX Figma

Botika — Pharmacy Management & Ordering System

Led a team of 4 to design a mobile pharmacy app that simplifies complex medication ordering into intuitive, friction-free interactions for patients and pharmacy staff.

Problem
Pharmacy ordering involved multi-step, error-prone manual processes; users struggled to navigate inventory and place orders efficiently.
Research
Conducted stakeholder interviews and task analysis to map pain points in the ordering journey; identified 6 critical drop-off points.
Process
Designed end-to-end user flows and wireframes; facilitated design reviews; balanced user needs against pharmacy business logic.
Outcome
Reduced ordering steps by ~40%; improved task completion clarity for first-time users through iterative wireframe testing.
ICarex · Web Platform
Frontend Lead Web Platform Alumni Tracking Figma

ICarex — Alumni Tracker & Relations Management

Designed and led front-end development for a responsive alumni management platform for the Institute of Computer Science, enabling networking, mentorship, and career tracking in one hub.

Problem
Alumni data was fragmented across spreadsheets; no central system for the institute to track employment, engagement, or communication.
Research
Interviewed institute staff and alumni to understand data needs, communication gaps, and feature priorities through structured interviews.
Process
Led mobile-responsive wireframing; collaborated on IA with client; ensured consistent branding and accessibility standards throughout.
Outcome
Delivered a production-deployed platform with verified alumni profiles, job board, and admin dashboard; coordinated multi-branch development.
SARI · Web App
UX/UI Designer Web App Student Tool Figma · React

SARI — Student Meal Planner for UPLB

Designed a budget-aware meal planning web app for university students, surfacing local food options and generating personalized meal combinations within a given daily budget.

Problem
UPLB students struggled to make meal decisions within tight budgets without knowing which nearby canteens offered value for their allowance.
Research
Surveyed students on eating habits and budget constraints; mapped decision-making patterns through contextual inquiry on campus.
Process
Designed budget-input flow, personalized meal suggestions, and establishment listing; conducted usability tests with target student users.
Outcome
Deployed live at sari-web-app.vercel.app; users can generate daily meal plans in seconds based on real local food data.
Donation App · Mobile
UX/UI Designer Mobile App Non-profit Flutter · Firebase

Donation Management Mobile App

Designed both donor and organization-facing flows for a donation platform supporting QR-based tracking, scheduling, and real-time notifications — bridging the gap between givers and non-profits.

Problem
Donors had no visibility into how donations were used; organizations lacked a unified tool to manage donors, schedules, and statuses.
Research
Identified two distinct user types (donors vs. org admins) with conflicting mental models; designed separate but complementary flows for each.
Process
Designed 90% of UI including QR-based status flows, scheduling screens, and notification system; iterated based on stakeholder feedback.
Outcome
Dual-role app enabling transparent donation tracking, org verification, and real-time status updates across donor and org dashboards.
Farm2Table · E-commerce
Team Lead E-commerce Web Platform React · Node.js

Farm2Table — Fresh Produce E-commerce Platform

Led design and front-end for a dual-sided e-commerce platform connecting local farmers to consumers — including product browsing, cart, order management, and a merchant admin dashboard.

Problem
Farmers lacked direct-to-consumer channels; buyers had no way to verify freshness or source of produce they ordered online.
Research
Mapped two user journeys (merchant vs. buyer); identified key trust signals needed at each stage of the purchasing funnel.
Process
Designed 95% of UI across customer-facing and admin views; optimized product listing IA and order fulfillment dashboard for efficiency.
Outcome
Full-stack deployed platform with intuitive dual-role experience; merchant dashboard reduced order management steps significantly.
DocuMentor · Accessibility Tool
UX/UI Designer Accessibility Gov-tech Figma

DocuMentor — Accessible Form Simplification Tool

Designed a tool that scans complex PDF government forms and automatically converts them into simplified, guided, step-by-step interfaces — removing bureaucratic barriers for older adults and people with disabilities.

Problem
Government and institutional online forms are inaccessible to older adults and users with disabilities due to complexity, dense layouts, and poor UX.
Research
Evaluated WCAG accessibility standards and conducted contextual research on how users with limited digital literacy interact with complex forms.
Process
Designed PDF scan-to-form transformation flow; applied progressive disclosure, plain language, and AI auto-fill features in the wireframe stage.
Outcome
Form completion time reduced significantly in user testing; participants rated the simplified interface as highly easier than the original PDF format.
